# Closed Erdős Problems

These are famous problems posed by Paul Erdős that have been solved.

## Problems

| Problem | Solved By | Year |
|---------|-----------|------|
| [Discrepancy Problem](./erdos-discrepancy-problem/) | Terence Tao | 2015 |
| [Erdős–Faber–Lovász Conjecture](./erdos-faber-lovasz-conjecture/) | Kang, Kelly, Kühn, Methuku, Osthus | 2021 |
| [Erdős–Heilbronn Conjecture](./erdos-heilbronn-conjecture/) | Dias da Silva, Hamidoune | 1994 |
| [Sumset Conjecture](./erdos-sumset-conjecture/) | Moreira, Richter, Robertson | 2019 |
| [Cameron–Erdős Conjecture](./cameron-erdos-conjecture/) | Sapozhenko, Green | 2003 |
| [Burr–Erdős Conjecture](./burr-erdos-conjecture/) | Choongbum Lee | 2017 |
| [Erdős–Graham Problem](./erdos-graham-problem/) | Ernest Croot | 2003 |
| [Distinct Distances Problem](./erdos-distinct-distances-problem/) | Guth, Katz | 2015 |

## About Solved Problems

These problems, some open for decades, have been resolved through breakthroughs in mathematics. Many solutions introduced new techniques that continue to influence research today.
